Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility. UniCheck is exclusively available to currently enrolled school students with an active School institutional email (*.sti.edu.ph).

2. Account Responsibility. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your credentials.

3. Purchases. All sales are final. Orders may be cancelled only while in "Pending" status.

4. Pricing. Prices are set by the Prowear campus store and subject to change without notice.

5. Delivery. Home delivery is subject to a shipping fee determined by the campus administrator.

6. QR Payments. Reference numbers must be entered accurately. Admin will verify before processing.

7. Changes. These terms may be updated at any time. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

Privacy Policy

Data We Collect. School email, name, phone, address, campus, and order history.

How We Use It. Solely for order processing, account management, and communications.

Storage. Stored securely in Google Firebase. We do not sell your data.

Your Rights. Update your information through account settings at any time.
